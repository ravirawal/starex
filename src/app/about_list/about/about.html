The Starex University, Gurugram, has been conceptualized under the provisions of the Haryana Private Universities (Amendment) Act 2016. The high-tech Millennium City - Gurugram is a milestone in the world of progress and growth. Starex University is set up on the Peri-Urban Suburbs of Gurugram.

Having a nice ambience of greenery, immaculate lawns, and a beautiful, well-maintained campus, it is sprawling in approximately 35 acres on a vast lush green stretch on Delhi - Jaipur Highway, NH-48. The meticulously developed impressive infrastructure of the University in Gurgaon, Haryana, equipped with all modern amenities, playgrounds, sporting facilities and world-class environment, predicts the fervour and passion of its faculty and team towards the distinction in every sphere of education that it strives to achieve.

The Starex University Derive to be an epitome of luminosity and excellence. It would impart the best of education to its students and prepare them to deal with the challenges of the professional world with zeal, high spirits, and optimism. Through world-class facilities and the most talented, dedicated, motivating & expert faculty, the university is committed to the betterment of its students. Also, it works to continuously inspire and drive the students to newer heights in learning and scholarship.
