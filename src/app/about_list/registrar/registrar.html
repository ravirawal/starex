Registrar Message

Mr. N.P. Singh

Registrar, Starex University.

Mr. N.P. Singh joined as Registrar of this University in January, 2025. Prior to assuming this role, he served in various esteemed organizations and brings him a wealth of experience in the field of Educational Administration. Over the course of more than 30 years in the field of Higher Education, he has worked extensively in establishment, administration, examination and evaluation.

His broad involvement in statutory bodies and committees (Board of Management, Finance Committee, Academic Council, Planning and Monitoring Board, Examination Committee, IQAC, Selection Committee, Departmental Promotion Committee, Recruitment and Promotion Rules framing Committee and Ordinance and Regulation framing Committee etc.) proves his strong commitment to the Higher Education. His active participation in these bodies across various universities and academic institutions underscores his deep understanding of academic governance and administrative responsibilities.
