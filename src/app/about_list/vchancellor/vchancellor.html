MESSAGE FROM THE DESK OF VICE CHANCELLOR

Prof.Dr. Madhu Parhar (Vice Chancellor)
Starex University, Gurugram, Haryana

Dear Students, we heartily welcome you to Starex University, Gurugram. As a responsible citizen, we hold firm expectations for the utmost ethical standards in conduct of Academic & Research compliance with all federal regulatory requirements, state laws and University policies. These standards are fostered through education, assessment and quality improvement. We set high standards for an environment rooted in honesty and respect for others by Teaching & Non-teaching staff member of Starex University.

At Starex, Exceptional research relies on diverse and complementary ideas. Across all institutional boundaries we promote team building creativity, and facilitates multidisciplinary, diverse teams to advance innovative and collaborative science.

We work to improve gender, race, ethnic and cultural representation in our Academic & Research workforce at Starex University. Diverse voices is not only the right thing to do, but it strengthens our Academic & Research capability and ensures focus on the topics and communities necessary to expand the benefits of Academic & Research to everyone.

Wishing you all the best.
