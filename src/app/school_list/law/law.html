School Of Law

Welcome to the School of Law, Starex University

Here at the School of Law, we blend the rigor of traditional legal education with the flexibility of digital learning. Our programs are designed for aspiring legal professionals who seek to balance their academic pursuits with personal and professional commitments.

Our curriculum is developed by experienced faculty members who are leaders in their respective fields. They bring a wealth of practical and theoretical knowledge to the virtual classroom, ensuring that you receive a robust and relevant education.

From foundational courses in constitutional law, contracts, and torts to specialized electives in international law, environmental law, and intellectual property, our programs cover a wide spectrum of legal disciplines.

We are committed to your success. Our support services include academic advising, career counseling, and technical support, ensuring that you have the guidance and resources necessary to achieve your goals.

Our Vision

At Starex School of Law, we have the vision to impart globalized education with the aim of producing world-class professionals, scholars, and academics in law and other disciplines.

The School of Law at Starex University aspires to build upon past experiences and current innovations in teaching to create an institution of global excellence for the future.

Join us at Starex University, Gurugram, where your legal education meets the future.
