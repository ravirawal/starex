A Spectrum of Programs for Every Passion

Are you captivated by the wonders of the physical world? Do you yearn to unlock its secrets through scientific exploration? Then look no further than the School of Physical Sciences (SOPS) at Starex University!

Starex University's SOPS is dedicated to fostering a vibrant community of learners and researchers who are passionate about pushing the boundaries of scientific knowledge. Our mission is to contribute to our understanding of the physical world through excellence in observational, theoretical, and experimental science.

Undergraduate Programs

B.Sc. (Non-Medical) Program (3 years)

The B.Sc. (Non-Medical) is a three-year undergraduate course focused on subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. This program is designed for students who have studied science without Biology at the higher secondary level. It offers a strong foundation in core scientific principles and prepares students for careers in research, teaching, IT, engineering, and more. Graduates can pursue further studies or professional courses to expand their career opportunities. This program is ideal for those interested in science and technology, excluding medical fields.
