Specialized B.Tech Programs

  • B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning: Delve into the world of AI and ML with a strong foundation in algorithms, data structures, and machine learning techniques. Our program, enhanced by academic support from Samatrix, prepares you to develop intelligent systems and drive innovation.
  • B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering: Gain a comprehensive understanding of computer systems, software development, and fundamental computing principles. This program provides a solid foundation for a diverse range of tech careers.
  • B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning with academic support of Samatrix: This specialized program, in collaboration with Samatrix, offers in-depth knowledge and practical experience in AI and ML, equipping you with the skills to excel in this rapidly growing field.
  • B.Tech in Cyber Security & Digital Forensics with academic support of Samatrix: Master the art of protecting digital assets and investigating cybercrimes with this specialized program. Gain hands-on experience and develop the skills to combat cyber threats.
  • B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning with academic support of IBM: This program, in collaboration with IBM, provides a unique opportunity to learn from industry experts and work on real-world AI and ML projects.
  • B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (Azure Data, Cloud & AI Developer) with academic support of Microsoft: Gain expertise in data, cloud, and AI development using Microsoft Azure. This program, in collaboration with Microsoft, prepares you for a successful career in cloud computing and data engineering.
  • B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (Cyber Security) with academic support of Microsoft: This program, in collaboration with Microsoft, focuses on cybersecurity, equipping you with the skills to protect digital infrastructure and combat cyber threats.
